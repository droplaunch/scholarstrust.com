About Scholars' Trust UAE

Scholars' Trust UAE is a youth-led, non-profit organization dedicated to empowering students across the UAE through mentorship, academic support, and leadership opportunities. Who We Are

Founded by passionate students, Scholars' Trust UAE brings together high schoolers and university students from diverse backgrounds. We believe in the power of peer support and the importance of sharing knowledge, experiences, and opportunities to help each other succeed.

What We Do

  • Mentorship: We connect students with mentors who guide them through academic choices, university applications, and personal development.
  • Workshops & Events: We organize interactive workshops, webinars, and camps on topics like university admissions, career planning, leadership, and more.
  • Community: Our platform is a safe space for students to ask questions, share resources, and build lasting friendships.
  • Opportunities: We regularly share internships, competitions, and volunteering opportunities to help students expand their horizons.

Our Vision

To foster a supportive and inclusive environment where every student in the UAE can access the guidance, resources, and inspiration they need to reach their full potential.

Get Involved

Whether you're a student looking for guidance, a mentor eager to give back, or an organization interested in collaborating, Scholars' Trust UAE welcomes you! Join our growing community and be part of a movement that uplifts and empowers youth across the country.
